Noodles

Description

Noodles are delicious. Make noodles.

Ingredients

  • A box of pasta
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Butter/Sauce of your choosing

Directions

  1. Boil generously salted water in a large pot
  2. Cook pasta in water until desired doneness is reached
  3. Strain pasta
  4. Stir in butter/sauce
